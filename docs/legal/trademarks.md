# Trademarks policy

OpenFamiliar may show plain-text names of interoperable tools (OpenAI, Gemini, xAI, Ollama, Codex CLI, Gemini CLI) in settings UI.

Forbidden in product identity:

- ChatGPT Pet, Gemini Familiar, Grok Dog, Codex Mascot
- Provider logos as part of OpenFamiliar branding
